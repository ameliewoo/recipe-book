Burrata Salad
Ingredients:1 ball burrata cheese,Cherry tomatoes,Basil leaves,Olive oil,Balsamic glaze
Cooking time: 10 minutes
Method:Arrange tomatoes and basil on a plate.Place burrata in the center.Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.Serve fresh.
Serving size: 2
Meal: Starter or Light Lunch
Dietary tags: Vegetarian, Gluten-free
