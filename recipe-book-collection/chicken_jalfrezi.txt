Chicken Jalfrezi
Ingredients:2 chicken breasts (diced),1 onion (sliced),1 bell pepper (sliced),2 garlic cloves,1 tbsp ginger,2 tbsp jalfrezi curry paste,400g chopped tomatoes,1 tbsp oil,Salt to taste
Cooking time: 30 minutes
Method:Heat oil in a pan and sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until soft.Add chicken and cook until browned.Stir in curry paste and cook for 2 minutes.Add bell pepper and chopped tomatoes. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.Season with salt and serve hot.
Serving size: 3
Meal: Dinner
Dietary tags: Gluten-free, Dairy-free
