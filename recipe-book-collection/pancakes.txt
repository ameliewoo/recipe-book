Pancakes
Ingredients:150g plain flour,2 eggs,300ml milk,1 tbsp oil,Pinch of salt
Cooking time: 20 minutes
Method:Whisk flour, eggs, milk, oil, and salt into a smooth batter.Heat a non-stick pan and pour in a ladle of batter.Cook until golden, flip and cook the other side.Repeat with remaining batter.
Serving size: 4
Meal: Breakfast, Dessert
Dietary tags: Vegetarian
