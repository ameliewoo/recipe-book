Greek Salad
Ingredients:1 cucumber - chopped,2 tomatoes - chopped,1 red onion - sliced,100g feta cheese - cubed,50g olives,2 tbsp olive oil,1 tsp oregano
Cooking time: 10 minutes
Method:Combine cucumber, tomatoes, onion, feta, and olives in a bowl.Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano.Toss gently and serve chilled.
Serving size: 2
Meal: Lunch
Dietary tags: Vegetarian, Gluten-free
