Chocolate Brownies
Ingredients:200g dark chocolate,175g butter,3 eggs,250g sugar,100g plain flour,30g cocoa powder
Cooking time: 35 minutes
Method:Preheat oven to 180°C (160°C fan).Melt chocolate and butter together.Beat eggs and sugar until fluffy.Fold in melted chocolate, then flour and cocoa.Pour into a lined baking tray and bake for 25–30 minutes.
Serving size: 8
Meal: Dessert
Dietary tags: Vegetarian
