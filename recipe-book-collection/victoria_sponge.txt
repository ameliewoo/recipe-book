Victoria Sponge
Ingredients:200g butter,200g sugar,4 eggs,200g self-raising flour,1 tsp baking powder,100g jam,100ml whipped cream
Cooking time: 40 minutes
Method:Preheat oven to 180°C (160°C fan).Cream butter and sugar, then beat in eggs.Fold in flour and baking powder.Divide into two tins and bake for 25 minutes.Cool, then sandwich with jam and cream.
Serving size: 8
Meal: Dessert
Dietary tags: Vegetarian
