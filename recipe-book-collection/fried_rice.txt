Fried Rice
Ingredients:2 cups cooked rice,1 egg,1 carrot - diced,1 spring onion - chopped,2 tbsp soy sauce,1 tbsp oil
Cooking time: 15 minutes
Method:Heat oil, scramble egg and set aside.Stir-fry carrot and onion.Add rice and soy sauce, mix well.Stir in scrambled egg and serve.
Serving size: 2
Meal: Lunch or Dinner
Dietary tags: Vegetarian
